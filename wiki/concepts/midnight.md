## Canonical definition
A privacy-focused blockchain built as a sidechain of Cardano, developed by Input Output Global (IOG). Every smart contract on Midnight executes via zero-knowledge proofs, making it a full-stack case study used throughout this book (Chapters 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 8, and 12).
